What we offer...

100% Refereed Games Integrity ✅

We love fun and spirited matches that's why all our games are refereed. And each ref is a certified match official with CSL and League1 refereeing experience.

7-A-Side Soccer Fast & Fun.

We play seven-a-side football -- one of the soccer variations with smaller feilds and goals with six outfield players plus keeper. The games are kinetic and generate great action for all positions. Check out our game rules here.

The Best Feild Turf Day or Night

Come play on FIFA grade feild turf -- the best in the region! Our feilds have distinguishing 7-a-side game lines, metal soccer goals, and industrial lighting for night time matches.
